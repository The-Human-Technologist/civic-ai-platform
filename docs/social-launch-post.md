## LinkedIn post

**Launching our public alpha: open-source civic intelligence for municipalities**

Cities already record video at junctions, markets, and schools — but most footage is only watched after something goes wrong.

We're open-sourcing the **AI Civic Operations & Road Safety Intelligence Platform**: a working demo that shows how municipalities and traffic departments could turn existing CCTV into **human-reviewed alerts** for potholes, waterlogging, waste hotspots, congestion, and advisory road safety — without automatic fines, facial recognition, or a surveillance stack.

**What's live today (honest scope):**
- Full Next.js dashboard + 30-day pilot proposal template
- Human review workflow (confirm / reject / field verify)
- Mock AI detections only — synthetic Barasat sample data
- Privacy-first defaults documented in the repo

**What's next:** Phase 2 real inference pipeline (YOLO/OpenCV, PostgreSQL). Contributors welcome.
